ENTREPRENEUR STORY

With the support of Danny Cruz (successful entrepreneur in New Orleans) and Vilexys Salas (award winning Realtor in NOLA and Metairie area), TACOS DEL CARTEL was conceived to disrupt the concept of Mexican Restaurants in the US.  Inspired in their love and respect for Mexican Culture, International Chef Julio Machado brings the best of Mexico to your table.

“After a lot of work, passion, hours of planning, designing, constructing, remodeling, decorating, cooking, tasting, training (and rescheduling after hurricanes, we are very happy and proud of what we have achieved! Thanks to all the people that made this possible…”
- Danny Cruz

TACOS DEL CARTEL IS 

We worked meticulously for over the last two years to build TACOS DEL CARTEL, agonizing and scrutinizing over every little detail. Every single item of our menu has being researched to make sure we are doing it the right way, including crafting our own signature cocktails. Being different starts when you enter the front door... from our stunning bar is bookended by hand-painted skeletons made by artist Danielle Hyne sitting on a glittering background to the skull-print glassware, you will have a memorable experience.

"The look of the restaurant started with our imagination and then we decided to hire Jacquelyn Lindsey of JL Studios. She created all of this with our ideas. We tried to make every spot Instagrammable: the bar, the murals, the bookcase…There's a good energy here."
- Danny Cruz
